The problem

Most offices are still routing patients through hold music.

Landlines and shared inboxes worked when an office had one location and a small staff. Across multiple offices, they create blind spots — nobody can see who's free, so calls go to voicemail and questions sit in an inbox.

Landline + email setup

  • ― Calls ring one desk, then go to voicemail
  • ― No visibility into who's available at other offices
  • ― Insurance questions wait for a callback or email reply
  • ― Covering a location short-staffed means physically transferring lines

FixedIT Tech setup

  • ✓ One number routes to the next available staff member, anywhere
  • ✓ Presence status is visible across every office in real time
  • ✓ A specialist can answer billing or insurance questions instantly
  • ✓ Any office can be covered remotely without new hardware
